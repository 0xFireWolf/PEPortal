Applications Management

Applications Management

One of the highlighted features of the new FireWolf MultiApp Launcher is applications management.

Now you can import any application to the launcher and categorize them.

Since most applications receive updates more frequently than the PE, you may import a newer version of your preferred app.

Besides, you can export your applications to a file for the next time you use PE.

We will discuss some details in this section.

This section is separated into the following segments:

  • Importing Your Applications (Basic)
  • Importing Your Applications (Advanced)
  • Exporting Your Applications and Others
