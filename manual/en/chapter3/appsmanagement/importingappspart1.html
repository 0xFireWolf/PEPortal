Importing Applications (Basic)

Importing Your Applications (Basic)

We will talk about how to import applications in this segment.

Method 1:

Click the Apps Management >> Importing Applications... menu and choose one or more applications in the dialog.

Method 2:

Click the Apps Management >> Manage Imported Applications.

Drag one or more applications to the list.

Method 3:

Click the Apps Management >> Manage Imported Applications.

Click the "+" button and choose one or more applications in the dialog.
