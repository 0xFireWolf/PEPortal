Restore the disk image

For both Windows and macOS users, we recommend you to use Etcher to restore the disk image to your disk.

Etcher is an open-source cross-platform image flash tool.

Open Etcher and click the Select Image button to choose your downloaded image.

Then choose your thumb drive or hard drive and click the Flash button.

Don't forget to backup files, because Etcher will erase your thumb drive.

Wait for a couple of minutes and that's it.
